My Favorite City

I am from the Philippines and my home city is my favorite place to be in.

Balanga City is the vibrant, rapidly developing capital of Bataan province in the Philippines, acting as its primary center for commerce, education, and government.

Recognized by UNESCO as a Learning City, it blends rich Spanish-colonial history with modern, sustainable urban planning.
Located ~124 km from Manila, it is known for its wetland conservation,, the stunning Balanga Cathedral, and as a key stopover for history and nature tourism.
